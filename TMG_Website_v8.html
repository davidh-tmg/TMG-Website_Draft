The Monday Group is a multi-family network dedicated to meeting the distinct financial goals of high-net-worth individuals and families. With a focus on wealth preservation and long-term growth, members invest across a wide spectrum of asset classes — including private and public markets, real estate, infrastructure, and venture capital.

TMG brings together a distinguished group of current and former public company CEOs, board members, Wall Street leaders, institutional CIOs, and seasoned entrepreneurs. With a collective net worth in the billions, members contribute deep expertise, substantial capital, and a shared commitment to supporting each other's success.
